### Advanced Capabilities

Beyond the basics, BeSimple offers deep integration with Electron to provide:
- **Direct FS Access**: No sandboxing limitations for your local files.
- **Monaco Power**: Full IntelliSense and multi-cursor support.
- **IPC Handlers**: Secure communication between main and renderer processes.
